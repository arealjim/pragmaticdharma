Retreat Finder

A curated directory of meditation and contemplative retreat sources

Aggregators & Marketplaces

Retreat Guru

Global
The largest retreat directory with 3,000+ centers worldwide. Search by tradition, location, date, and price. Centers self-list.

BookRetreats

Global
Booking marketplace with 5,000+ retreats. Includes reviews, price comparisons, and detailed inclusions lists. Trip-advisor-style experience.

BookYogaRetreats

Global
Large yoga and meditation retreat booking platform. Sister site to BookMeditationRetreats. Filters by destination, style, and duration.

Insight Timer Retreats

Global
Retreat directory within the popular meditation app. Teachers list their own retreats. Broad traditions from secular mindfulness to vipassana.

Retreats Online

Primarily US
Directory covering yoga, meditation, wellness, and spiritual retreats. Searchable by location, type, and date.

dharma.org

US Insight centers
Cross-center calendar aggregating retreats from IMS, Spirit Rock, and affiliated Insight Meditation centers. Clean, structured data.

Vipassana & Insight Meditation

Dhamma.org Dana

200+ centers worldwide
S.N. Goenka's global Vipassana network. Standardized 10-day courses, all donation-based. The single largest source of structured retreat data.

Insight Meditation Society

Barre, MA
Premier US insight meditation center. Residential retreats from weekends to 3 months. Teachers include Joseph Goldstein and Sharon Salzberg.

Spirit Rock

Woodacre, CA
Major West Coast insight center. Residential and day-long programs. Teachers include Jack Kornfield. Offers BIPOC and young adult retreats.

Insight Retreat Center

Santa Cruz, CA
Dedicated insight meditation retreat center in the Santa Cruz mountains. Offers silent residential retreats in the Theravada tradition.

Cloud Mountain

Castle Rock, WA
Multi-tradition retreat center in the Pacific Northwest. Hosts teachers from insight, Zen, Tibetan, and other contemplative traditions.

Southern Dharma

Hot Springs, NC
Retreat center in the Blue Ridge Mountains. Multi-tradition programming including insight meditation, Zen, and yoga.

Gaia House

Devon, UK
Major UK insight meditation center. Full retreat calendar with renowned international teachers. Silent residential retreats.

Zen & Chan

Plum Village

France, US, Asia
Thich Nhat Hanh's global network of monasteries and practice centers. Mindfulness retreats with an emphasis on community and engaged Buddhism.

San Francisco Zen Center

San Francisco, CA
One of the oldest US Zen centers. Three locations: City Center, Green Gulch Farm, and Tassajara (remote mountain monastery with summer guest season).

Zen Mountain Monastery

Mt. Tremper, NY
Mountains and Rivers Order. Offers sesshins (intensive Zen retreats), introductory weekends, and residency programs in the Catskill Mountains.

Upaya Zen Center

Santa Fe, NM
Zen center founded by Roshi Joan Halifax. Programs bridging Zen practice with social action, chaplaincy, and end-of-life care.

Dharma Drum Retreat Center

Pine Bush, NY
Chan (Chinese Zen) tradition founded by Master Sheng Yen. Silent illumination and huatou retreats from beginner to advanced level.

Tibetan Buddhism

Shambhala

200+ centers worldwide
Global network offering meditation programs rooted in the Shambhala Buddhist tradition. Structured path from beginner through advanced practice.

FPMT

160+ centers worldwide
Foundation for the Preservation of the Mahayana Tradition. Gelug Tibetan Buddhist centers with study programs and meditation retreats.

Tergar

Global
Mingyur Rinpoche's meditation community. Joy of Living program and retreat offerings blending traditional Tibetan practice with modern accessibility.

Tara Mandala

Pagosa Springs, CO
Nyingma Tibetan Buddhist retreat center founded by Lama Tsultrim Allione. Dakini practices, Chod, and meditation retreats in a mountain setting.

Drala Mountain Center

Red Feather Lakes, CO
Formerly Shambhala Mountain Center. Multi-tradition retreat center in the Rockies. Meditation, yoga, and contemplative arts programs year-round.

Theravada Monasteries

Abhayagiri Dana

Redwood Valley, CA
Thai Forest tradition monastery in Ajahn Chah's lineage. Offers periodic guest retreats and a monastic residency program. Fully donation-based.

Amaravati Dana

Hertfordshire, UK
Thai Forest monastery in Ajahn Chah's lineage. Group and family retreats, day visits, and monastic life. All offerings are free.

Birken Forest Monastery Dana

British Columbia, Canada
Thai Forest Theravada monastery in a remote forest setting. Silent retreats, monastic visits, and personal retreat stays.

Bhavana Society Dana

High View, WV
Theravada forest monastery and retreat center founded by Bhante Gunaratana. Meditation retreats and personal retreat opportunities.

Multi-Tradition & Wellness Centers

Omega Institute

Rhinebeck, NY
Major holistic learning center on 250 acres. Hundreds of workshops and retreats spanning meditation, yoga, wellness, creativity, and personal growth.

Kripalu Center

Stockbridge, MA
One of the largest yoga and wellness retreat centers in North America. Year-round programs from yoga teacher training to mindfulness retreats.

Esalen Institute

Big Sur, CA
Iconic human potential center on the Pacific coast. Workshops blending meditation, bodywork, psychology, and the arts since 1962.

Art of Living Retreat Center

Boone, NC
Blue Ridge Mountain retreat center. Breathwork, meditation, yoga, and Ayurveda programs rooted in the Art of Living tradition.

Garrison Institute

Garrison, NY
Multi-tradition venue in the Hudson Valley that hosts retreats from many different teachers and organizations. Focus on contemplative practice and social change.

Hollyhock

Cortes Island, BC, Canada
Retreat center on a remote island off British Columbia. Programs in mindfulness, leadership, yoga, and creative expression.

1440 Multiversity

Scotts Valley, CA
Modern retreat campus in the redwoods. Programs with leading thinkers in mindfulness, wellness, relationships, and personal development.

Vallecitos Mountain Retreat

Taos, NM
Contemplative retreat center in the Carson National Forest. Multi-tradition programming with an emphasis on nature, silence, and social justice.

Individual Teachers

Meditate with Tucker

Various US locations
Tucker Peck's retreat schedule. Pragmatic dharma and insight meditation retreats with a focus on direct experience and clear instruction.

Tara Brach

Washington, DC area
Psychologist and meditation teacher offering retreats on mindfulness and self-compassion. Author of Radical Acceptance.

Shinzen Young

Various / Online
Unified Mindfulness system. Retreats combining scientific rigor with contemplative depth. Known for systematic meditation techniques.

Leigh Brasington

Various US/Europe
Specialist in jhana (deep concentration) practice. Retreats focused on developing the meditative absorptions described in the Pali Canon.

Directories & Resources

BuddhaNet

Global
Long-running Buddhist information network. Global directory of centers organized by country and tradition. Some listings may be dated.

DharmaNet

Primarily US
Buddhist information network with a center and retreat directory focused on North American offerings.

The Buddhist Society

UK
Directory of UK Buddhist centers and retreats across all traditions. The oldest Buddhist organization in Europe, founded 1924.

Dharma Overground

Online community
Pragmatic dharma discussion forum. Not a listing site, but a rich source of retreat recommendations and reviews from experienced practitioners.
